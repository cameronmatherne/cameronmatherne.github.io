About me

I have been bartending for 3 years and I truly have a passion for it. I love crafting a cocktail, handing it to someone, and being fully confident that they will absolutely enjoy it. I feel like there is an art in that. At these private events, I am able to mix and create any drink with the ingredients I am provided with. Hiring me for your events will take the stress of pleasing guests off of your shoulders and allow you to sit back and enjoy the event as much as them. My goal is to provide delicious, hand-crafted cocktails, along with fast and entertaining service.

A little more about myself... I was 16 years old when I first started working in the service industry. My first job was working as a bar-back at Le Pavillion, a local reception venue. During my days as a bar-back, I always admired the bartenders. Not because they were my bosses, but because they genuinely enjoyed what they were doing. Their enthusiasm, the way they engaged with guests, crafted fantastic drinks, and exuded confidence - they were the life of the party. I bar-backed there for two years until the day I turned 18. I got my bar card, and I was ready to dive into bartending. I continued bartending at Le Pavillion for another year and absolutely loved it. A few months before turning 19, I landed a position at Ruffino's on the River. To earn my spot behind the bar, I started as a busboy and bar-back while still juggling shifts at Le Pavillon. I even spent my 19th birthday bar-backing at Ruffino's on a lively Saturday night. My commitment to becoming a seasoned bartender was unwavering. One day, a family friend asked if I could work as a bartender for a "private event." Excitedly, I agreed, partly driven by the need for extra cash. While working this private event, I was approached by guests wondering if I had business cards. I hadn't considered business cards or taking private events seriously until that moment. The questions about them during the event sparked something in me. The day after, thoughts of starting my own company flooded my mind. Over a year later, I've turned that inspiration into reality. I am now a private event bartender, combining my knowledge of mixology with fast and entertaining service.

How it works

- Reserve a time with me

- Submit deposit of $100 to secure your event date. Deposit will be applied to total event rate.

- Client provides bar area with a bar, rented or with tables put together. This can be indoor or outdoor.

- Client provides beer, wine, liquor, mixers, ice chests to ice down beer/wine if needed and cups. *Rocks, flutes, and wine glasses are available to rent for up to 50 guests.

- I provide all bar equipment necessary along with fruit and garnishes. I provide straws, napkins, and an ice chest with ice for drinks. *See FAQ for specific equipment.

- I answer any questions the client may have prior to the event.

- I show up an hour early to set up for the event and the party gets started!

FAQ

What equipment/services do you provide?

I provide bar mats, shakers, strainers, all fruit along with a fruit tray, bar rags, bottle openers, and even a cocktail smoking kit. I also provide straws, napkins, and an ice chest with ice for drinks. I am able to make any cocktail with the ingredients I am provided with.

How much do you charge?

I charge a $30 set up fee, then a flat rate of $60/hour. Final rates may change based on location, duration, and size of event.

How long have you been bartending?

I have been bartending for 3 years now, working in restaurants, event venues, along with my own private events.

How early can I book an event?

I can book events as far in advance as needed, and will always do my best to accommodate on short notice.
