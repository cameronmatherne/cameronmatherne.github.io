My Story

I was 16 years old when I first started working in the service industry. My first job was working as a bar-back at Le Pavillon, a local reception venue. I worked there for a year, until I turned 18 and could get my bar card to step into the bartending scene. During my days as a bar-back, I always admired the bartenders. Not because they were my bosses, but because they genuinely enjoyed what they were doing. Their enthusiasm, the way they engaged with guests, crafted fantastic drinks, and exuded confidence - they were the heartbeat of the party. The day I turned 18, I got my bar card, and I was ready to dive into bartending. I continued working at Le Pavillon another year, bartending. A few months before turning 19, I landed a position at Ruffino's on the River. To earn my spot behind the bar, I started as a busboy and bar-back while still juggling shifts at Le Pavillon. I even spent my 19th birthday bar-backing at Ruffino's on a busy Saturday night. My commitment to becoming a seasoned bartender for both Le Pavillon and Ruffino's was unwavering. One day, and a family friend asked if I could work as a bartender for a "private event." Excitedly, I agreed, partly driven by the need for extra cash. While working this private event, I was approached by guests wondering if I had business cards. I hadn't considered business cards or taking private events seriously until that moment. The questions about them during the event sparked something in me. The day after, thoughts of starting my own company flooded my mind. Over a year later, I've turned that inspiration into reality. I am now a private event bartender, combining mixology knowledge with fast and entertaining service.

How it works

Once a time is reserved, we can arrange a meeting for me to answer any questions you may have prior to the event. On the day of the event, I will arrive an hour before the scheduled time to prepare my bartending station. The client provides a bar area, for example a designated space at the location of the event, tables if needed, ice chests, beer, wine, and ice for cocktails. The client provides all alcohol mixers, cups, napkins, and I provide all bartending equipment. I charge by the hour; Once you book with me, I will be willing to work an event as long as neccessary. Additionally, no event is too soon, and it is never to early to book with me. So let's get this party started and book with Bronson!
