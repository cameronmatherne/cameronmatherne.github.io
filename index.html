My Mission

" To relieve the host of stress by ensuring that all guests have a drink in their hand and are having a great time! "

How it works

Reserve your time with me by submitting a $100 deposit, which will be applied to your total event rate. I'll ensure your event is seamless, whether it's an indoor or outdoor gathering. I can work with a provided bar area, a rented setup, or tables arranged to create the perfect bar experience. Before the event, I'm happy to answer any questions to ensure everything runs smoothly. On the day, I’ll arrive an hour early to set up, so your party is ready to kick off on time!

      FAQ

      What equipment/services do you provide?

      I provide bar mats, shakers, strainers, all fruit along with a fruit tray, bar rags, bottle openers, and even a cocktail smoking kit. I also provide straws, napkins, and an ice chest with ice for drinks. I am able to make any cocktail with the ingredients I am provided with.

      How much do you charge?

      I charge based on the duration of your event, the location, and how many guests will be attending. Contact me today for a free quote!

      How long have you been bartending?

      I have been bartending for 3 years now, working in restaurants, event venues, along with my own private events.

      How early can I book an event?

      I can book events as far in advance as needed, and will always do my best to accommodate on short notice.
