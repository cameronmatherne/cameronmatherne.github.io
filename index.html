      FAQ

      What equipment/services do you provide?

      We provide bar mats, shakers, strainers, all fruit along with a fruit tray, bar rags, bottle openers, and even a cocktail smoking kit. We also provide straws, napkins, and an ice chest with ice for mixing and making drinks. We am able to make any cocktail with the ingredients provided.

      How much do you charge?

      We charge based on the duration of your event, the location, and how many guests will be attending. Contact us today for a free quote!

      How long have you been bartending?

      Our team brings over 12 years of combined bartending experience, honed through working in restaurants, event venues, and hosting unforgettable personal events.

      How early can I book an event?

      We can book events as far in advance as needed, and will always do our best to accommodate on short notice.
