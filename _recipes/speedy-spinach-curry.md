title: "Speedy Spinach Curry"
image: speedy-spinach-curry.jpg

tags:
- mains
- vegetarian

ingredients:
- 1 onion
- 2 tablespoons green curry paste
- 7 ounces spinach
- ½ cup roasted cashews
- 1 block of tofu

directions:
- Heat a large frying pan on medium-high.
- Add the onion and cook, stirring, until it’s soft.
- Stir in the curry paste and cook for 1 minute.
- Add the spinach and tofu, and stir for 5 minutes.
- Top with cashews before serving.
