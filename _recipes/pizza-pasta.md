ingredients:
  - 1 box (8-12 oz) high-protein pasta (chickpea, lentil, or other protein-enriched pasta)
  - 1 cup pizza sauce sauce (low-sugar)
  - 1 cup cooked lean ground turkey
  - 1/2 cup part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded
  - 1/4 cup sliced black olives (optional)
  - 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  - 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - Fresh basil leaves for garnish
directions:
  - Cook the protein pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  - While the pasta cooks, heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and cook the ground turkey until browned. Add garlic powder, black pepper, and half of the Italian seasoning.
  - Add onions to the skillet and cook 3-4 minutes until slightly softened.
  - Stir in the pizza sauce and let simmer 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  - Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine.
  - Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, cover for 1-2 minutes until cheese melts.
  - Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and remaining Italian seasoning.
