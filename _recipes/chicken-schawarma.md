ingredients:
  - 1.5 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  - 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  - 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  - 1 small red onion, sliced
  - 2 tbsp plain Greek yogurt
  - 2 tbsp lemon juice
  - 2 tsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp ground cumin
  - 1 tsp ground coriander
  - 1/2 tsp turmeric
  - 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  - 1/2 tsp onion powder
  - 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/2 tsp sea salt (adjust to taste)
  - Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish
directions:
  - Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a large sheet pan with parchment paper.
  - In a large bowl, whisk together Greek yogurt, lemon juice, olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin, coriander, turmeric, garlic powder, onion powder, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  - Add chicken strips to the marinade and toss until evenly coated. Let sit for at least 10 minutes (or up to 2 hours in the fridge).
  - Spread the marinated chicken, bell peppers, and onion evenly on the sheet pan in a single layer.
  - Roast in the preheated oven for 20–25 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
  - Optional: broil for 2–3 minutes at the end for extra crispiness.
  - Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro before serving. Serve hot with a side of cauliflower rice or a simple salad.
---

A **high-protein, low-calorie sheet pan chicken shawarma** that's packed with flavor! The Greek yogurt marinade keeps the chicken tender while the spices give it that classic shawarma taste. Easy, healthy, and perfect for weeknight dinners.  

Optional: serve with a squeeze of lemon or a dollop of low-fat tzatziki for extra freshness.
