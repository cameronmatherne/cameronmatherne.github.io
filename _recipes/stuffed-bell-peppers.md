ingredients:
  - 4 large bell peppers, tops cut off and seeds removed
  - 1 lb lean ground turkey or chicken
  - 1/2 cup cooked quinoa or cauliflower rice
  - 1 small onion, finely diced
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 cup diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp cumin
  - 1/2 tsp chili powder
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/2 tsp sea salt (adjust to taste)
  - 1/2 cup shredded low-fat cheese (optional)
  - Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish
directions:
  - Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  - In a skillet over medium heat, sauté onion and garlic until soft, about 3-4 minutes.
  - Add ground turkey (or chicken) to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon, and cook until browned and cooked through.
  - Stir in cooked quinoa (or cauliflower rice), diced tomatoes, smoked paprika, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2-3 minutes to combine flavors.
  - Spoon the mixture evenly into the prepared bell peppers and place them upright in a baking dish.
  - Optional: sprinkle shredded low-fat cheese on top.
  - Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 5-10 minutes until peppers are tender and cheese is melted (if using).
  - Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro before serving.
---

A **protein-packed, low-calorie dinner** that’s colorful, flavorful, and filling. Lean ground turkey or chicken boosts protein while bell peppers provide fiber, vitamins, and natural sweetness. Perfect for meal prep or a healthy family dinner.
