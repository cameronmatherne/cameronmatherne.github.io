ingredients:
  - 1 lb lean ground beef (90% lean or higher)
  - 1 small onion, diced
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced
  - 1 lb protein pasta 
  - 1 cup low-sodium beef or chicken broth
  - 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  - 1/2 cup shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1 tsp chili powder
  - 1/2 tsp black pepper
  - 1/2 tsp salt

directions:
  - In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef with diced onion and garlic until the beef is browned and cooked through. Drain excess fat if needed.
  - Add pasta to the skillet, stirring to combine.
  - Pour in the broth, sprinkle in smoked paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well and bring to a simmer.
  - Reduce heat and cover for 8-10 minutes, pasta is cooked through.
  - Remove from heat and stir in Greek yogurt and shredded cheddar cheese until creamy and well combined.
  - Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot.

A **healthy twist on classic Hamburger Helper**, this version is high in protein, low in calories, and packed with veggies. Perfect for a weeknight dinner that fills you up without the guilt!

You can swap cauliflower rice for whole wheat pasta if you want more carbs, or add extra spices for a flavor kick. Enjoy a comforting, creamy meal **without sacrificing your nutrition goals**.
