ingredients:
  - 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast
  - 1 1/2 cups salsa (mild, medium, or hot)
  - 1/2 cup chicken broth
  - 1 tsp garlic powder
  - 1 tsp onion powder
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1/2 tsp cumin
  - 1/2 tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  - 1/2 tsp black pepper
  - Optional:
    - 1/2 tsp chili powder
    - Juice of 1/2 lime
    - Fresh cilantro for garnish
directions:
  - Place the chicken breasts in the bottom of the slow cooker.
  - Sprinkle garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, black pepper, and chili powder (if using) evenly over the chicken.
  - Pour the salsa and chicken broth over the chicken.
  - Cover and cook on Low for 7-8 hours or High for 3â€“4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shreds with a fork.
  - Shred the chicken directly in the slow cooker using two forks.
  - Stir the shredded chicken into the sauce to coat evenly.
  - Add lime juice if using, garnish with cilantro, and serve hot.
---

Juicy, **high-protein shredded chicken** cooked low and slow with salsa and broth for extra moisture and flavor. Perfect for meal prep, tacos, burrito bowls, salads, or low-carb wraps.
