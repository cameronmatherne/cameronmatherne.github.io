ingredients:
  - 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  - 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  - 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  - 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  - 1 medium red onion, sliced
  - 2 tbsp olive oil
  - 1 tsp chili powder
  - 1 tsp smoked paprika
  - 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  - 1/2 tsp onion powder
  - 1/2 tsp cumin
  - 1/4 tsp black pepper
  - 1/2 tsp sea salt (adjust to taste)
  - 1 tbsp lime juice
  - Optional: fresh cilantro and lime wedges for garnish
directions:
  - Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a large sheet pan with parchment paper.
  - In a large bowl, combine olive oil, chili powder, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  - Add sliced chicken, bell peppers, and onion to the bowl and toss until everything is evenly coated with the seasoning.
  - Spread the mixture in a single layer on the sheet pan.
  - Roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
  - Remove from the oven and drizzle lime juice over the top.
  - Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately. Optional: serve with low-calorie tortillas or over cauliflower rice for a lighter option.
---

A **quick, protein-packed weeknight dinner** that’s full of flavor! Chicken provides lean protein, while colorful bell peppers add fiber and vitamins. Sheet-pan cooking keeps it simple and cleanup minimal. Perfect for meal prep or a fast, healthy dinner.
